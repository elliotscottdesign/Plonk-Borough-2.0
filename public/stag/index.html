The Stag · Centurion Julianus

of Legio IX · Polonia Victrix

Commander of morale. Slayer of sobriety. Reclaimer of Eagles.

The day has come, the end of Julian's single life. As we begin celebrating his marriage, I'm delighted to send him off surrounded by his best mates.

As a keen history fan and lover of all things Roman, it only seemed right to dress him as a Centurion and give him his marching orders. Armed with nothing but a helmet, a belly full of meat, plenty of wine, and the mission of having a fucking great time.
